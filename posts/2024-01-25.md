When will all of your problems ever be solved? You know what the answer is.

The best defense of why we study little things:

                Both big and little things exist. We can make rules about big things, but those rules need to be
                EXPLICIT as to how they affect the little things we can observe. Otherwise they don't mean much at all.
                This is not a diminishment.

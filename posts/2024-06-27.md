There is a never-ending conversation we have with advertisements in which you may
                        reply once or twice a year. You are made to consider a peculiar relationship, the one which you
                        have with your toothpaste or your swimsuit or your car brakes, in the captivity of reading or
                        listening. Then you might go to the store (or not) and in a couple months the seasonal reports
                        come in. And then they think, "I shouldn't have put a comma there". That is how a new thing is
                        created, as new as any conversation can be.

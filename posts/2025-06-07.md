I've detached this website from my professional identity, which means I will get less visits.
                But hopefully people can come here to stop and think.

You equate many forms of abstraction with one another, causing you to hallucinate opportunities where
                there are none ...When such shamanic impulses existed in settler days their incoherent napkin diagrams
                at least proposed rockets which pointed towards the sky, whereas your own ambitions are pointed
                variously at the nearest shopping mall, parking lot, or a more handsome-looking piece of shrubbery. In
                this way, you confuse the social and aesthetic joys of modern travel with the pure aesthetic happiness
                of wandering up and down well-worn roads.

I want to hold pieces of paper of varying quality dear to my heart.

Family Feud : hungry mouths all trying to reach the center of something. Hoping that in a
                spark of intuition or a lemma of rational thought there is a meal to keep them alive.

I love the dirty ones more than the minced, in that I respect
                extravagant expression more than reserved. I admire reserve, especially when
                taken to an ascetic nth...<i>Horse</i>, then, unhorses what is not horse. -- C.D. Wright

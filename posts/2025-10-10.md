(10.10.2025) <i>Adrienne Rich</i> Most, if not all human lives are full of fantasy -- passive
                day-dreaming which need not be acted on. But ... even to think well, is not fantasize... there has to be
                an imaginative transformation of reality which is in no way passive. to question to challenge, to
                conceive of alternatives, perhaps to the very life you are living at that moment. nothing can be too
                sacred for the imagination to turn into its opposite or to call experimentally by another name. For
                writing is renaming.

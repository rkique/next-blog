(10.21.2025)
            <pre style="font-size: 9px;">
rabbits emerged costume-like underground from 
dorms clothed in silky fabrics articulate wishes
in tastes of congregation: shady alcoves and restive trees

the truly correct dare not unclasp their hoods
impervious to MORNING RUSH: pleading caffeinated 
exceptionalism.
jigsaw puzzle pieces, all of us, waiting to be passed over

paradise must be tinged with sorrow, 
interiority and anthropological conversion lost
to the scattered frissons of complete quietude

gorgeous? georgic? gem-like identities. An oaken wood box
for preservation of curiosities? A heart-rending cry
and the continuity of the spirit
            </pre>

You wanted to hear about the future, but I could only tell you about the past. You wanted to know whether I sold methods, to which I could only reply who's asking? I am still not in my painting. I am a figure of speech. Speech detached from words. Speech which does lazy arcs around its dinner plate. I hope I get to the good part soon. To lie in wait is infinitely better than waiting in line.

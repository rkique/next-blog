### Machine translation
Natural language evolves rapidly and doesn't have well-defined structure
Rule-based approaches vs godless DL approach
 - hard to scale to more languages
 - translations depend on context

P(w_i) =  P(w_i | w_-1, w_-2, w_-3...)
P(w_i) =  P(w_i | Source sentence,w_-1, w_-2, w_-3...)

source sentence --> encoder --> sentence embedding --> decoder (embed -> sentence) --> output sentence

- too many sentences to onehot embed.

- to generate sentence embedding, we need an encoder
- use an LSTM
- feed in source sentence
- take final LSTM state as sentence embedding
- take the sum of LSTM

input is sentence in one language, output is sentence in other language

precision: how many retrieved elements are relevant?
recall: how many relevant elements were retrieved?

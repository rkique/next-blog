#### JSON.stringify

JSON.stringify will convert simple objects to and from strings. They can handle objects, strings, arrays, and booleans. Construct specially chosen objects, passing them to `JSON.stringify` in `serialize`, convert back to intended JS values in `deserialize`.

Can write an incomplete solution via `JSON.stringify` and `JSON.parse`. Can also serialize values using the `toString()` method.

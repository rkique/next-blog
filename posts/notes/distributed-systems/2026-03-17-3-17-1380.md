##### Task & Data Parallelism

Data parallelism means:
- do the **same operation**
- on **different partitions of the data**
- at the same time
    


Task parallelism means
- do **different operations**
- at the same time
- often because they do not depend on each other yet

- Each grep (task) executs after the others are done.
- Compilation server can dynamically track dependencies
- Allow independent regions to execute in parallel.

grep `user1` in1 in2 > out1
grep `user2` in3 in4 > out2
grep `error` out1 out2 > out

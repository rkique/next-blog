###### Apply 
Software supply chain: dependencies can have vulnerabilities.

Example instances:
- Leftpad
- Event-stream
- SolarWinds: largest data breach ever. 
	- privileged access to gather performance data and other information from logs generated by customer IT assets.
- From Russian Intelligence Service: SVR.

- MCP with CLI. 

**Approach: Decomposition**

Turn your own code into a 'security-aware distributed system'.

Security: disallowing disallowed behaviors
At a high level, we want CIA. 
**Confidentiality**: Protect against unauthorized reads. The idea of security is fundamentally a social one: who is able to read? Who is allowed to understand or take from an existing system?

**Integrity**: Protect against unauthorized writes. The idea of integrity is fundamentally a social one: who is allowed to write? Who is allowed to document as a part of a system. 

**Availability**: Protect against too much execution (resource exhaustion). Prevent Denial of Service Attacks.

Denial of Service Attacks
- Distributed attacks via botnets.
- Regular-expression parsing
- Memory-safety vulnerability

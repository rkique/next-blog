Analytic languages: sequences of free morphemes.
Synthetic language: bound morphemes attach to other morphemes.
- agglugination: morphemes and boundaries are easy to identify, come apart well.
- fusional: do not come together well.
Isolating languages: do not use affixes to compose words.

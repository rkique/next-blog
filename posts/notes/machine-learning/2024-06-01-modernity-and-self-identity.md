#### guilt and shame
- Some argue that guilt is a private anxiety-state, and shame is public one
	- I am much more attuned to the shame axis than I am of guilt
	- But shame depends on feelings of personal insufficiency which may not need to be publicly observed
		- Guilt Axis: discrete acts relating to violation of codes or taboos, cumulative processes in which autonomy is developed by surmounting repression, wrongdoing towards loved or respected others, trust based on absence of betrayal

		- Shame Axis: overall tissue of self-identity, insight into the nature of the narrative of self-identity, inadequate for loved or respected others, trust based on being "known" by the other, where self-revelation does not incur anxieties over exposure
	- "overt" shame and "bypassed" shame
		- overt shame is humiliation
		- bypassed shame is unconsciously experienced anxieties about inadequacies of self

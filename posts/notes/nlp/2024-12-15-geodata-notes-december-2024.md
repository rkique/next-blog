student working on long-term research project: identify the grounds of schools. Vector layer for the grounds of the schools. PlanetLabs 4-band image of schools, sr-corrected. City of Providence GIS hub: parcel boundaries for the city. Supervised, Unsupervised classification.

Data in this folder consist of satellite imagery in a GeoTIFF format from Planet Labs that cover the bounding-box area of Providence, Rhode Island on two dates: April 23, 2024 and November 13, 2024. This is 3-meter resolution,  4-band imagery with RGB and NIR (near-infrared) that has been corrected for surface reflectance. Each date comes in two formats: the standard Planet Labs version, and a version that was normalized to Sentinel 2 bands for consistent radiometry.

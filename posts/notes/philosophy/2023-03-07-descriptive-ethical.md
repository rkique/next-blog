### Karmo's law
There is no sound argument whose conclusion is ethical and premises non-ethical.

alternative logic system: relevance
(P and not P) -> Q

### essentially ethical inference: a kind of vocabulary occurs essentially in a valid argument 
iff you can make it invalid by replacing all instances of the expression with some other expression; otherwise, it occurs INESSENTIALLY.

Elizabeth is my mom. Jack is Elizabeth's brother. Therefore, jack is my uncle.
examples of essential ethical expressions: IS, MY, (MOM/DAD), BROTHER, UNCLE

Socrates is a man. All men are mortal. Socrates is mortal.
essential ethical expression: ALL

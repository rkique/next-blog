The reason why I became fixated on word vectors was that they had a power of unpredictability to them. Words are incredibly compressed, forceded meaning -- vectors have a power to them that is like a summer storm. But deep learning is not about creating vectors, it's about making predictions. And making predictions means being right. It requires a set of questions and a set of answers. It requires predictability. 

Vectors are the essence of all that is unpredictable and violent and messy about a language. And their utilization is only as stopgap for the predictable and the totally routine. It is harnessing lightning in a bottle to power grainmills. I love the violence and the totally adrift. I love the magic.

Can I make room in my heart for something less? It's hard when all you've done seems to tell you that it can go on. The blessing today is that it can go on. But not forever.
